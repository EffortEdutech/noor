# NOOR Language Terms Lock

## Talab an-Noor

`Talab an-Noor` means **The Seeking of Light**.

Use it for the guided AI-supported study workspace where the user asks NOOR to generate reflection, Ishraq notes, or lesson preparation from verified Quran, tafseer, and related sources.

## Short label

When space is limited, use:

```text
Talab
```

